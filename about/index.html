About

I am the Founder and Managing Director of Hack and Craft.

Hack and Craft creates Industry 4.0 technology for organisations in the industrial, logistics, and manufacturing sectors. Our technology has driven innovation in companies such as Schneider Electric, Jaguar Land Rover, AkzoNobel, EDF, and RS Components.

I studied Philosophy and Artificial intelligence and have mentored at Google for Entrepreneurs and the Microsoft Tech accelerator. I am also a fellow of the Royal Society for the Encouragement of Arts, Manufactures and Commerce and a Founder/Trustee of Brighton Table Tennis Club.
